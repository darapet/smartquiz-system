🧩 How to Play

Puzzle Battle — understand the game before you start

🎮 4 Game Modes
⚡ Quiz Battle
Pick the correct answer from 4 choices (A/B/C/D). The faster you answer, the more points you earn. As soon as you pick, you get instant feedback — correct/wrong — plus an AI explanation!
🔤 Word Hunt
A clue is shown and you type the hidden word. Blank cells show how many letters. A free hint letter is revealed every 8 seconds. Type your answer and press Enter or tap →
📝 Crossword
Across and Down clues appear on screen. Tap a clue to select it, then type the answer. Solve as many as you can before time runs out — most solved wins!
🧩 Jigsaw Quiz
Answer 9 questions to unlock all 9 puzzle pieces on your 3×3 board. Each correct answer fills one piece. Complete your board before everyone else to win!
📋 Step-by-Step
1
Host creates a room — picks subject, game mode, number of questions and time per question, then clicks "Create Room".
2
Share the 6-letter room code — friends open puzzle.html, switch to "Join Room", type the code and their name, then tap "Join Room". No account needed!
3
Host taps "Start Game" — XZILY AI generates all questions (takes ~5–10 seconds). Everyone gets the same questions at the same time.
4
Play! — Answer as fast as you can. Scores update live for everyone. Chat with players on the right side.
🏆
Highest score wins! — Full leaderboard and podium shown at the end.
💡 Scoring & Tips
⚡ Speed bonus — correct answers earn up to +5 extra points if answered quickly
🔥 Combo bonus — 3 correct in a row earns +3 bonus points
❌ No penalty — wrong answers don't remove points, always try!
⏰ No answer — if time runs out without picking, that question scores 0

You can play solo or with up to 10 friends at the same time
